<h3 data-pm-slice="1 1 []"><strong>Challenges in the Aerosol Market</strong></h3>
<ul data-spread="false">
<li>
<p><strong>Environmental Concerns:</strong>&nbsp;Traditional aerosol propellants, such as hydrocarbons and fluorocarbons, contribute to air pollution and climate change.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Stringent Regulations:</strong>&nbsp;Governments worldwide are enforcing strict regulations on the use of chemicals in aerosol formulations, requiring manufacturers to adopt eco-friendly alternatives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Raw Material Costs:</strong>&nbsp;The rising cost of raw materials, including aluminum and steel for cans, impacts production expenses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Recycling Challenges:</strong>&nbsp;While progress is being made, recycling aerosol containers remains a challenge due to their pressurized nature.</p>
</li>
</ul>
