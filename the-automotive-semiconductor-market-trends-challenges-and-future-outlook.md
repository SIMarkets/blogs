<h3><strong>1. Global Chip Shortage</strong></h3>

<p>Designing and manufacturing automotive-grade semiconductors requires high investment in research, development, and compliance with stringent safety standards.</p>

<p>Automotive semiconductors must withstand extreme temperatures, vibrations, and electromagnetic interference, making production complex and challenging.</p>
