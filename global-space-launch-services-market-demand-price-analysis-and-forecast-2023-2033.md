<h3>What are Space Launch Services?</h3>
<p>Space launch services encompass the systems and processes required to transport payloads&mdash;such as satellites, space probes, and crewed spacecraft&mdash;into orbit or beyond. These services are provided by a mix of government space agencies (e.g., NASA, ESA, Roscosmos) and private companies (e.g., SpaceX, Blue Origin, Rocket Lab). The market includes a wide range of launch vehicles, from small rockets designed for microsatellites to heavy-lift rockets capable of carrying large payloads or crewed missions.</p>
