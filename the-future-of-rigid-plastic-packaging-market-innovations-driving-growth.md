<h3>What are Rigid Plastic Packaging Systems?</h3>
<p>Rigid plastic packaging refers to packaging solutions made from hard or semi-hard plastic materials that retain their shape and provide structural integrity. Common examples include bottles, containers, trays, clamshells, and caps. These systems are widely used across industries due to their lightweight nature, durability, and ability to preserve the quality of products.</p>
<p>Rigid plastic packaging is typically made from materials such as polyethylene terephthalate (PET), polypropylene (PP), high-density polyethylene (HDPE), and polystyrene (PS). These materials are chosen for their strength, chemical resistance, and recyclability.</p>
