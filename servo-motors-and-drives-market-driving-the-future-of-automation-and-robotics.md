<p><strong>What Are Servo Motors and Drives?</strong><br />Servo motors are high-performance electric motors designed for precise control of angular or linear position, velocity, and acceleration. They are paired with servo drives, which act as the brain of the system, controlling the motor&rsquo;s operation based on feedback from sensors. Together, they form a closed-loop system that ensures exceptional accuracy and responsiveness.</p>

Servo motors and drives are essential for achieving the precision required in these applications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Growth in Robotics and CNC Machines</strong><br />The surge in industrial robotics and computer numerical control (CNC) machines has fueled the demand for servo systems. These applications require high-speed, high-accuracy motion control, which servo motors and drives excel at providing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Energy Efficiency and Sustainability</strong><br />Modern servo systems are designed to be energy-efficient, reducing power consumption and operational costs. This aligns with the global push toward sustainable manufacturing practices.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Advancements in Technology</strong><br />Innovations such as integrated servo motors, compact designs, and smart drives with IoT capabilities are driving market growth. These advancements make servo systems more versatile and easier to integrate into complex automation setups.</p>
</li>
</ol>
